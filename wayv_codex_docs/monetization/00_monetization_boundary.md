# wayv Monetization Boundary

## Core Principle

wayv does not monetize by increasing emotional dependency, social pressure, or fear of missing out.  
wayv may monetize storage, usage capacity, customization, and gentle control, but basic safety and the right to rest must remain available to free users.

wayv는 감정적 의존, 사회적 압박, 놓칠까 봐 생기는 불안을 키워 수익화하지 않는다.  
wayv는 보관, 사용량, 개인화 설정, 조용한 제어권을 유료화할 수 있지만, 기본 안전 장치와 쉬어갈 권리는 무료 사용자에게도 제공되어야 한다.

## Allowed Monetization Areas

| Area | Free | Paid | Boundary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Private traces | limited daily traces and/or recent trace storage | more traces, longer retention, archive | must not expose private traces |
| Private notes | shorter note length or limited count | longer notes, more saved notes | no emotion analytics dashboard |
| Wave posting | limited daily waves | higher daily wave limit | no spam incentive |
| Trace-to-wave draft | limited daily conversions | more conversions | no auto-publish |
| Rest mode | basic rest mode free | advanced schedule/topic-specific rest controls | basic rest cannot be paid-only |
| Wave follow-up notifications | off/basic occasional | custom rhythm and categories | no real-time popularity alerts |
| Persona listening room | small free trial | limited paid usage | no unlimited dependency loop |
| Export | basic manual copy | structured export/archive | no public validation score |
| Theme/customization | basic | extra gentle visual preferences | no gamified status cosmetics |

## Forbidden Monetization

- Selling visibility boosts.
- Paid ranking, paid trending placement, or paid "more people see your wave" mechanics.
- Author analytics that reveal private resonance, private traces, private notes, or inferred emotional impact.
- Private trace insights for authors.
- Emotional vulnerability scoring.
- Unlimited AI companion or persona loops designed to maximize dependency.
- Paywalling basic safety, basic rest mode, or basic moderation protection.
- Gamified streaks, FOMO notifications, or "your wave is popular" monetization.
- Ad targeting based on private traces or private notes.

## Non-Binding V1 Model

Free may include limited waves per day, limited private traces per day, recent trace storage up to a reasonable cap, basic rest mode, basic safety/moderation, and minimal or off-by-default wave follow-up notifications.

Paid may include more private traces, longer trace retention, more wave drafts/conversions, more daily waves, advanced rest rhythm, topic-specific quiet filters, structured export/archive, and limited persona listening room usage if that product is later built.

Must remain free: basic account safety, moderation protection, basic rest mode, delete/export request path, ability to remove private traces, and ability to cancel before publishing.

## Persona Listening Room Boundary

Allowed: listening, reflection, validation, short grounding, and non-intrusive presence.

Forbidden: advice, coaching, therapy claims, diagnosis, action planning, behavior optimization, dependency maximization, unlimited use, and manipulative retention loops.

Required limits:

- Daily turn limit.
- Session time limit.
- Cooldown after session.
- Clear "not counseling or therapy" positioning.
- Crisis/safety fallback.
- User can stop easily.

Potential product names:

- 조용한 방
- 잠시 들어주는 곳

Avoid names such as AI 상담, AI 코치, AI 치료, or AI 멘토.
